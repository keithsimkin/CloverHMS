# Product Overview

## Hospital Management System (HMS)

An open-source desktop application for comprehensive hospital operations management. HMS provides digital patient records, appointment scheduling, staff management, inventory tracking, complete clinical workflows from patient arrival through discharge, financial management, emergency services, blood bank management, and quality assurance.

## Core Capabilities

### Clinical Operations
- **Patient Management**: Digital records with demographics, medical history, allergies, and emergency contacts
- **Appointment Scheduling**: Calendar-based scheduling with provider availability and conflict prevention
- **OPD/IPD Management**: Separate outpatient and inpatient department workflows
- **Clinical Workflows**: Integrated symptom recording, diagnosis management, and prescription system with ICD-10 codes and drug interaction warnings
- **Patient Flow**: End-to-end tracking from registration → triage → consultation → treatment → pharmacy → billing → discharge
- **Bed Management**: Bed allocation, status tracking, visual floor plans, and occupancy rates

### Medical Services
- **Laboratory Services**: Test ordering, sample tracking, results entry, and report generation
- **Pharmacy Management**: Medicine inventory, prescription fulfillment, and dispensing tracking
- **Blood Bank**: Donor management, blood inventory by type, donation tracking, and expiry alerts
- **Emergency Services**: Ambulance management, emergency call logging, case tracking, and response time monitoring
- **Operation Management**: Surgical procedure tracking, operation theater scheduling, and outcome reporting

### Financial Management
- **Billing & Payments**: Comprehensive billing with multiple payment methods
- **Insurance Management**: Provider management, policy verification, and claim processing
- **Advance Payments**: Deposit tracking and application to bills
- **Expense & Income Tracking**: Categorized financial tracking with reports
- **Hospital Charges**: Service pricing configuration and management
- **Payroll Processing**: Staff salary management with allowances, deductions, and bonuses

### Administrative Functions
- **Staff Management**: Employee profiles, role-based access control, schedule tracking, and department organization
- **Inventory Management**: Medical supplies tracking with low-stock alerts and transaction history
- **Document Management**: Secure document storage with versioning and access control
- **Communication System**: Notice board, internal messaging, and staff schedules
- **Service Packages**: Bundled service offerings with package pricing
- **Quality Management**: Patient feedback, complaint tracking, and resolution monitoring

### Reporting & Analytics
- **Dashboard**: Real-time metrics for patients, appointments, beds, inventory, and finances
- **Clinical Reports**: Birth certificates, death certificates, and operation reports
- **Financial Reports**: Income statements, expense reports, payment analysis, and payroll reports
- **Operational Reports**: Appointment statistics, patient demographics, staff utilization, bed occupancy, and emergency response times

## User Roles

- **System Administrator**: Full system access including user management and configuration
- **Hospital Admin**: Hospital-wide management, reporting, and staff management
- **Doctor**: Patient records, appointments, clinical documentation, prescriptions, operations
- **Nurse**: Patient care documentation, vital signs, bed management, medication administration
- **Receptionist**: Patient registration, appointment scheduling, OPD/IPD management
- **Lab Technician**: Laboratory test management, sample collection, results entry
- **Pharmacist**: Medicine inventory, prescription fulfillment, dispensing
- **Accountant**: Financial management, billing, payments, payroll, reports
- **Inventory Manager**: Supply management and inventory transactions
- **Read-Only Viewer**: View-only access across all modules

## Design Philosophy

Modern dark theme with professional color palette focused on clarity and efficiency in clinical environments. Desktop-first approach with responsive design for tablet use in clinical settings.

### Color Palette
- **Rich Black (#0f131a)**: Primary background for deep contrast
- **Gunmetal (#232c3d)**: Secondary surfaces and card backgrounds
- **Prussian Blue (#273043)**: Interactive elements and borders
- **Cool Gray (#9197ae)**: Secondary text and muted content
- **Mint Cream (#eff6ee)**: Primary text for excellent readability
- **Imperial Red (#f02d3a)**: Error states and critical actions
- **Rojo (#dd0426)**: Error emphasis and warnings

The color scheme provides AAA accessibility compliance with high contrast ratios, ensuring readability in various lighting conditions common in healthcare settings.

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter - Used for body text, forms, tables, and general UI elements
- **Heading Font**: Mona Sans - Used for page titles, section headings, and navigation
- **Accent Font**: Poppins - Used for buttons, labels, and emphasis text

The font combination provides excellent readability in clinical settings while maintaining a modern, professional appearance.
